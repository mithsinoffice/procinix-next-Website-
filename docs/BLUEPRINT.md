# Procinix Website Blueprint

Source of truth for the www.procinix.ai rebuild. Updated 2026-04-20.

## 1. Objective

The website must do 6 jobs at once: position Procinix clearly, explain full platform coverage, show enterprise credibility, convert visitors into demos/assessments, support global SEO + geo landing pages, and show measurable value through ROI / value assessment. This is a growth, credibility, and conversion engine — not a brochure.

## 2. Core Positioning

- **Brand line:** Automation & Beyond
- **Hero line:** Don't Change Your Business to Fit Software.
- **Positioning:** Procinix is a unified finance operations platform that goes beyond automation — combining agentic AI, intelligent workflows, real-time analytics, and measurable business outcomes across Source-to-Pay, Order-to-Cash, and Record-to-Report.
- **Enterprise fit:** Built for multi-entity, multi-country, and multi-industry operations.

## 3. Product Architecture — Three Finance Families

### A. Source-to-Pay (S2P)
Sourcing · Procurement · Accounts Payable · Payments · Travel & Expense · Petty Cash · Vendor Reconciliation · Budgeting / Spend Control · Fixed Assets

### B. Order-to-Cash (O2C)
Order Management · Billing & Invoicing · Accounts Receivable · Collections · Customer Reconciliation

### C. Record-to-Report (R2R)
Month-End Close · Cash Flow Visibility · Consolidation · Provisions & Accruals · Amortization · Year-End Close

These are the backbone — not side mentions.

## 4. Primary Audience

**Business / Finance Leadership:** CFO, Finance Director, Controller, Head of Shared Services, Head of Transformation, Head of Procurement, Head of AP / AR
**Operating Teams:** Procurement leaders, AP managers, AR / collections leaders, Record-to-report teams, Finance transformation teams

**Buyer concerns the site must answer:**
- Will this adapt to our complexity?
- Can it handle multi-entity and multi-country?
- Can it improve DSO / controls / close efficiency?
- Is it just automation, or real intelligent operations?
- What business outcomes will it drive?
- Can I trust it at enterprise scale?

## 17. ROI / Value Assessment (flagship — treat as a product)

**Name it:** "Finance Transformation Value Assessment" or "Procinix Value Realization Engine" — NOT "calculator".

### Flow
1. Select finance family (S2P / O2C / R2R)
2. Select module under family
3. Enter realistic operating inputs

### Inputs

**Always-required org inputs:** number of entities · number of FTEs for function · avg annual loaded cost per FTE · % effort on selected process/module.

**S2P module inputs (by module):** RFQs/PRs/POs/invoices/payment runs/expense claims/month · reconciliation cases/month · exception / rework indicators.

**O2C module inputs:** orders/month · invoices/month · overdue accounts / open items · collection touches · reconciliation cases · annual revenue · credit period · current DSO · target DSO.

**R2R module inputs:** entities in scope · close cycles/year · close tasks per cycle · reconciliation tasks · journals / packs / entries · current close days · target close days.

### Outputs

**Always:** Monthly Savings · Annual Savings · FTE Capacity Released
**Conditional:** DSO Improvement + Cash Released (O2C) · Close Days Reduced (R2R) · cycle-time / accuracy uplift.

### Output layers
Module output → Family output → Enterprise summary (combined total impact).

### Required disclaimer
> "Estimates are directional and depend on workflow maturity, process design, data quality, automation scope, and user adoption."
